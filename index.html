About Me

I was born on Febuary 11, 1983 in San Francisco California. I lived in San Franscico until I was 10 and spent the rest of my childhood/teen life in a city called Hercules. You may think its a huge city by the name of it, however it's just a small city where everyone knows each other. During my teens my hobbies were creating music, graphic designing, basketball, snowboarding, and fishing, however I took art and music more seriously. After high school I then atteneded a junior college which didn't pan out the way I wanted it to so I ended up getting full time job for few years until I decided to go to The Conservatory of Recording Arts and Sciences in 2011. Music has always been my passion and by attending the school it allowed me to be a better audio engineer and gave me exposer to the industry.

After I graduated I interned at a professional studio called The Mix Room. There I learned how studios operate on a day to day basis. I also had the opportunity to meet knowledgable engineers in the industry who gave me a few pointers as I sat in their sessions. After my intern I became a freelance audio engineer for a few years, however it was not consistent, so I wanted the change my career paths. Being a creative and technical person, I wanted to try something new so I started my journey into becoming a web development.
